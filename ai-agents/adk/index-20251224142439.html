UX Thinking Behind Each Variant

  1. Minimalistic Card: Prioritizes readability and quick information consumption. Clean layout with high contrast ensures job details are easy to scan, suitable for mobile-first contexts.
  2. Background Image Card: Adds visual context and brand ambience. Overlay gradients keep text legible while giving depth, appealing to users who enjoy storytelling.
  3. Hover Interaction Card: Provides subtle feedback on interaction, improving perceived responsiveness. The lift effect signals clickability, useful on desktop interfaces.
  4. Rating Badge Card: Highlights company reputation instantly, aiding trust. Badges draw the eye and can influence decision-making for candidates valuing employer culture.
  5. Split Layout Card: Efficient use of horizontal space, separates logo and details visually. Good for wider screens where side-by-side layout enhances scanning speed.
