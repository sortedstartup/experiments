UX Thinking: Learning Paths

  • Path-as-a-Commitment: Instead of simple courses, these are "Paths" with specific career outcome data.
  • Decision Metrics: For each path, the UI explicitly calls out the "Mastery Duration" (e.g., 75 days) and social proof to reduce choice paralysis.
  • State Awareness: The Enrolled path is visually distinct (Indigo border) and replaces the action button with "Resume" to minimize friction.
